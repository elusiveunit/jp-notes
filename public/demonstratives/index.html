Demonstratives

Words used to indicate which entities are being referred to and to distinguish those entities from others

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demonstrative

Groups of words with a similar ko-so-a-do pattern.

  • こ: close to the speaker (e.g. “this dog”)
  • そ: close to the listener (e.g. “that store”).
  • あ: far from both speaker and listener, but that can be seen or understood (e.g. “that mountain over there”).
  • ど: questioning what or which (e.g. “what car?”)
